~Directions~

  • Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Break up noodles and toss into boiling water. Cook until tender, about 3 minutes.
  • Stir in chicken, sour cream, seasoning packet, and dill. Simmer until heated through. Serve garnished with green onions.
